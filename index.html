Main Features

Reduced eye strain

Dark mode reduces the amount of light emitted by screens, which can help reduce eye strain, particularly in low-light conditions.

Battery saving

For devices with OLED or AMOLED screens, dark mode can help save battery life by illuminating fewer pixels.

Aesthetic appea

Many users find dark mode aesthetically pleasing and feel that it makes the content stand out more.

Buttons

Dark/light mode functionality allows users to switch between a dark color scheme, often with light text on a dark background, and a light color scheme, typically with dark text on a light background, providing customization based on user preference and environmental conditions.
